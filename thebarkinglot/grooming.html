    Why Should You Consider Our Professional Dog Grooming Services?

    • We Do It All

      We assure you that we will do the brushing, bathing, drying, trimming, or clipping with clean,sterile brushes and clipping blades. We also clean your pet’s ears and check for signs of infection. When their coat has dried, we trim, clip, or shave your pet if necessary. We also have other services, such as teeth brushing, custom cutting, and styling.

    • We Have The Appropriate Equipment

      We have all the appropriate tools to make your pet look his best, including trimmers, rounded scissors, and an adjustable grooming table. We provide safe, gentle shampoos for dogs with skin sensitivities or irritations, including those with mites. We also know how to groom for breed show cuts, which takes great care and understanding of the breed’s special needs. We will guarantee that your dog gets the appropriate cut and style.

    • We Are Experts In Handling Pets

      We are experts in handling elderly or anxious pets when brought to grooming facilities. Our groomers are trained to be gentle and to handle your pet with the utmost care and consideration. We can handle them softly and confidently and ensure they have the best grooming experience.

    • We Have A Welcoming and Fully Equipped Facility

      Washing a pet, trimming, drying, and combing hair coats can be time-consuming. But we can work around your schedule to get your dog in for a wash and expert styling. All you have to do is drop off your pet and pick it up when it’s done. We offer pet-friendly hotels where your pet can stay until you pick it up later.

    Here at The Barking Lot, we specialize in expert pet grooming services in Dubai. To book an appointment for your pup, please contact us online or call us at +971 50 454 2275.
